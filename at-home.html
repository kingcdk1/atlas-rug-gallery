How It Works

Four steps, zero pressure.

Step 01 Tell us the room

Share a few photos and measurements. We curate three to five options for your space, in the right size and palette.

Step 02 We deliver free

Complimentary local delivery within the DFW metroplex. We place the rugs for you and answer any questions you have.

Step 03 Live with it

Walk on it. See it in morning light. Show the kids. Move the furniture around. Take your time. No rush.

Step 04 Keep or return

Keep the ones you love. We collect the rest. No restocking fees, no headache, no awkward conversations.
