How It Works

Four ways to take one home.

Option 01 In Person

Reserve your paddle, enjoy hors d'oeuvres and wine at preview, then bid live from the floor on auction night.

Option 02 Phone Bidding

Pre register with us and bid by phone with a dedicated bidder assigned to your lots.

Option 03 Online Live

Log in and bid in real time from anywhere in the country. Stream the auction, see every lot.

Option 04 Absentee Bid

Leave a maximum bid and let us execute on your behalf. Simple, private, no bidding war stress.

What We Auction

Pieces with provenance.

Our auctions feature estate rugs from across the country, consignments from private collectors, and inventory from retiring dealers. Every lot is inspected, condition reported, and photographed in detail before it hits the catalogue.

You will see everything from fine city weaves and museum quality antiques to unusual tribal pieces and decorative modern rugs at accessible price points. Something for the collector and something for the first time buyer.
