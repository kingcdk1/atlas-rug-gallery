Cleaning & Restoration

Every rug leaves brighter than it came in.

We wash rugs the traditional way. Full submersion, hand scrubbed with mild plant based soap, rinsed until the water runs clear, then hung and air dried. The fibers come back soft, the colors come back awake, and the weave lasts another generation.

Whether it is a family heirloom or a daily driver from the living room, we treat every rug like it matters. Because to the family that owns it, it does.

Our Services

Four ways we bring rugs back.

  1. 01

    Pet Stain & Odor Removal

    Urine, vomit, accidents. We neutralize the odor at the source and lift the stain without bleaching the wool or breaking down the foundation. Pet owners, this is what you need.

  2. 02

    Hand Washing

    Cold water submersion, hand scrubbed with soft bristle brushes, rinsed thoroughly, then slow dried flat. The way rugs have been cleaned for centuries.

  3. 03

    Moth & Pest Treatment

    Full eradication of moth larvae, carpet beetles, and other pests, along with protective treatment to prevent return. If you see casings or tunnels, bring it in.

  4. 04

    Restoration & Reweaving

    Fringe repair, edge binding, reweaving of worn sections, and foundation rebuilds by hand. We treat antique rugs like the heirlooms they are.

Restoration

One stitch at a time.

Restoration is patient work. A hole may take weeks. A frayed side may need a new foundation. We work with masters who have spent decades doing this, matching wool by weight and color, rebuilding the weave so the repair is nearly invisible.

If your rug has seen better days, bring it in for an honest assessment. Some repairs cost less than you think. Some rugs are worth the investment. We will tell you which is which.
