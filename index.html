Since 1986 Forty Years

Hassan's gallery. Still family run.

Atlas Rug Gallery opened on Camp Bowie Boulevard in 1986. Hassan Khoshrou, a first generation Persian immigrant with a deep love for the rugs of his homeland, built the gallery on a handshake guarantee: every rug on the floor would be exactly as described. No inflated provenance. No pressure sales.

Forty years later, Hassan's three sons carry on what he built. Three generations of North Texas families have trusted this gallery with their floors, their heirlooms, and their homes. We are still here. Still pouring tea. Still telling stories.
