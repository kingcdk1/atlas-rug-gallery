Our Collection Antique Modern

Two centuries, one floor.

From village looms to contemporary studios, our inventory spans generations of craft. Every piece is hand selected, hand washed, and inspected before it reaches the gallery floor.

Antique Rugs

Stories already woven in.

Persian, Turkish, Caucasian, and tribal weavings with decades of patina. Hand knotted in natural wool and vegetable dye, chosen for rarity, color, and the story already woven into the pile.

The best antique rugs become more beautiful, and more valuable, with age. We specialize in city weaves from Isfahan, Tabriz, and Kashan, alongside tribal pieces from the Caucasus and Anatolia.

City Weaves

Isfahan, Tabriz, Kashan, Nain, Qum.

Tribal & Village

Heriz, Kazak, Serapi, Bakhtiari.

Vegetable Dye

Natural plant and mineral colors.

Provenance

Full history and documentation.

Modern Rugs

Clean lines. Warm floors.

Contemporary weaves that feel clean without feeling cold. Hand knotted and hand tufted pieces from today's best workshops, built with the same wool and the same patience as the antiques.

We carry everything from oversized neutrals for open concept homes to bold geometrics built for families and everyday life. Fade resistant, stain resistant wool, and pile depths that feel right under bare feet.
